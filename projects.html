Industrial Documentation: Cement Factory

üè¢ Lensa Smanda üìç Cilacap, Indonesia üé¨ Documentary

Overview

A professional documentary created with Lensa Smanda organization. This project documents the operations of a famous cement factory in Cilacap.

Documentation Focus

The documentary captures the cement production process from raw materials to final product. It showcases the factory's operations, technology, and the workforce behind this essential industry.

Production

  • On-site industrial filming
  • Professional video techniques
  • Interview coordination
  • Technical process visualization

Challenges

Filming in an active industrial facility was challenging due to limited access. We had to rely on restricted areas, follow strict safety rules, and make the most of the available visual resources to capture meaningful footage despite the limitations.

Skills Developed

This project enhanced my documentary filming skills, taught me professional documentation techniques, and showed me how to tell compelling stories about industrial processes.
